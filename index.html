About Doç. Dr. Ender Cem Bulut

Doç. Dr. Ender Cem Bulut is an Associate Professor of Urology at Gazi University Medical Faculty, specializing in advanced urological procedures including robotic surgery, laser treatments, and minimally invasive techniques. He leads cutting-edge research in kidney stone treatment, prostate surgery using Thulium fiber laser, and MRI-fusion prostate biopsy procedures.

Medical Services

Comprehensive urological care tailored to your needs

MRI-Fusion Prostate Biopsy

Advanced targeted biopsy using MRI-transrectal ultrasound fusion technology for precise prostate cancer detection with reduced pain and anxiety through innovative techniques.

Thulium Fiber Laser Surgery

Cutting-edge Thulium fiber laser technology for kidney stone treatment and benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) surgery. Leading research project (2022-2024) advancing minimally invasive laser techniques.

Percutaneous Nephrolithotomy

Expert in both supine and prone position PCNL procedures. Research-proven techniques comparing pain levels, analgesic requirements, and hospitalization time for optimal patient outcomes.

Robotic Urological Surgery

Advanced robotic-assisted laparoscopic procedures including radical prostatectomy, nephrectomy, and adrenalectomy. Expertise in da Vinci surgical system for precision and minimal invasiveness.

Uro-Oncology Research

Specialized research in prostate cancer, testicular tumors, and renal cortical tumors. Published expertise in castration-resistant prostate cancer treatment and oncological surgical outcomes.

Retrograde Intrarenal Surgery (RIRS)

Flexible ureteroscopy and laser lithotripsy for complex kidney stone cases. Expanding indications and advanced endoscopic techniques for optimal stone-free rates with minimal morbidity.
